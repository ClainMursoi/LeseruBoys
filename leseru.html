About Us

AIC Leseru Boys Secondary School, founded in the year 1983 is a Boarding Boys, County level Secondary school located in Turbo Sub-County, Uasin Gishu County, Kenya. Under the leadership of Principal Daniel Loponoki assisted by his Deputy Mr.wilson Kimani Misoi, we strive to equip our students for life.

Mission: To equip students for life.

Vision: Sustainable education for empowerment.

Motto: Hard work to success.

Subjects Taught

  • Languages:
    • English: Focuses on grammar, literature, and communication skills.
    • Kiswahili: Emphasizes grammar, literature, and effective communication.
  • Sciences:
    • Chemistry: Covers organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry.
    • Physics: Includes mechanics, electricity, magnetism, and modern physics.
    • Biology: Focuses on botany, zoology, human anatomy, and environmental science.
  • Humanities:
    • Geography: Studies physical and human geography, map reading, and environmental conservation.
    • History: Explores local and world history, with emphasis on significant historical events and figures.
    • Christian Religious Education (CRE): Provides moral and spiritual education based on Christian teachings.
  • Social Sciences:
    • Business Studies: Includes entrepreneurship, accounting, economics, and management.
    • Agriculture: Covers crop production, animal husbandry, agribusiness, and sustainable farming practices.
  • Mathematics: Focuses on algebra, geometry, calculus, and statistics.

Academic Resources

  • Laboratories:
    • Chemistry Laboratory: Equipped with modern apparatus for conducting experiments in organic and inorganic chemistry, titrations, and chemical analysis.
    • Physics Laboratory: Features equipment for experiments in mechanics, electricity, optics, and thermodynamics.
    • Biology Laboratory: Includes microscopes, dissection kits, and specimens for studying botany, zoology, and human biology.
  • Library: The school has a well-equipped library with a wide range of textbooks, reference books, academic journals, and digital resources. It provides a conducive environment for study and research, supporting the students' academic needs across all subjects.

Teaching Staff

Leseru Boys School has around 20 qualified and dedicated teachers. Each teacher is specialized in their subject area, ensuring that students receive high-quality instruction and personalized attention. The teachers are committed to fostering a supportive and challenging learning environment.

Sports & Societies

Sports

Leseru Boys School offers a variety of sports to promote physical fitness and teamwork among students. These include:

  • Football: The school has several football teams, providing opportunities for both competitive and recreational play.
  • Basketball: The school features a well-maintained basketball court and organizes regular matches and tournaments.
  • Athletics: Students can participate in track and field events, including sprints, long-distance running, and field events like javelin and shot put.
  • Volleyball: The school has a volleyball team that competes in inter-school tournaments.
  • Swimming: With access to a swimming pool, students can engage in swimming activities and competitions.

Societies and Clubs

Leseru Boys School encourages students to join various societies and clubs to develop their interests and skills beyond the classroom. These include:

  • Debate Club: Provides a platform for students to hone their public speaking and critical thinking skills through debates and public speaking competitions.
  • Science Club: Encourages students to explore scientific concepts through experiments, projects, and participation in science fairs.
  • Drama Club: Offers opportunities for students to express their creativity through acting, playwriting, and stage production.
  • Environmental Club: Promotes environmental awareness and sustainability through various activities like tree planting and recycling projects.
  • Music and Choir: Students can develop their musical talents through participation in the school choir and music lessons.
